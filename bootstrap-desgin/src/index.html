THE RETURN DRAWS CLOSER.

A spectacular reimagining of one of the most visionary games ever, FINAL FANTASY VII REMAKE rebuilds and expands the legendary RPG for today.


Join Cloud Strife, a former member of Shinra’s elite SOLDIER unit now turned mercenary, as he lends his aid to the anti-Shinra organization, Avalanche, unaware of the epic journey that awaits him.


The FFVII Remake combines incredible visuals, stunning world-design, a glorious musical score, seamless strategic action-based combat and a captivating cast of characters.


Assess your enemies' strengths and weaknesses, and employ powerful magic and special abilities to fight effectively through Midgar in a mix of traditional command-based combat and real-time action.

CHARACTERS.

Cloud Strife.

An ex-SOLDIER: first class, Cloud came to Midgar to start a new chapter of his life as a mercenary.

Aerith Gainsborough.

A flower seller who lives in the Sector 5 slums, Shinra has kept a close eye on Aerith ever since she was a child.

Tifa Lockhart

Although a loyal member of Avalanche, Tifa is not convinced that bombing mako reactors is the solution.

Barret Wallace

The leader of an independent Avalanche cell in Midgar, Barret was disavowed by the old guard for his extreme methods.
